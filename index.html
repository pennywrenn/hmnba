What this is

The playbook has always existed.

Thurgood Marshall had one. The architects of Dobbs had one. The drafters of Project 2025 published theirs openly. The asymmetry in American civic life is not that one side has a playbook and the other doesn’t.

It is that one side has been cooking from written recipes for a century while the other side has been improvising from vibes and good intentions.

This site is the written recipe. It is organized like a food blog — because a recipe is a set of operations with a defined sequence, a list of ingredients, an expected yield, and proof that someone already made it in their own kitchen. Every recipe on this site is anchored in an outcome that has already happened somewhere.

The site does not tell you what to believe. It tells you how to do what you already believe needs doing.

The shift is from user to operator. It starts here.
